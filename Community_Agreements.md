# Community Agreements #

## For Entire Class ##
- be confident to share your thoughts; having grace/consideration
- be respectful when others are speaking
- not defaulting to a single answer/mode of thinking
- question your own answers/thinking
- be willing to change perspective
- culture of accountability
	- listen to feedback
- speak from the “I”
- ask questions
	- not knowing is ok; admitting it is how we learn
- stories stay, lessons leave
- listening to understand, not respond
- respect beliefs; approach with curiosity
- be willing continue conversations outside of class

## For Teaching Team ##
- organized about expectations and where things live
- adapt to different learning styles
- give feedback in a timely way
